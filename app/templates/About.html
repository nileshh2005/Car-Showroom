About Us

Car-Showroom Advantage

India’s pre-owned car market is growing and maturing in tandem, and Car-Showroom is at the forefront of it. Our sole and overriding objective is to empower both the pre-owned car buyers and sellers through a methodical integration of technology and industry experience.

About Car-Showroom

Since foraying into India’s pre-owned car market in 2001, Car-Showroom channel for pre-owned cars, has grown in both size and stature. Being among the first major organized player in the market, the brand has expanded its pan India reach via a network of over 558 outlets across 279 cities. Car-Showroom has now become the marketplace of choice for pre-owned car buyers and sellers, catering to their diverse needs across the country.

Why Car-Showroom

When you choose Car-Showroom, you get:
  • High-quality & reliable pre-owned cars, digitally evaluated on 376 checkpoints
  • Fair & transparent prices on pre-owned cars from multiple brands
  • Exemplary services & convenient processes for both the buyers & sellers

Transparency at Car-Showroom

Car-Showroom believes that the experience of buying a pre-owned car shouldn’t be any different from that of buying a new car. To offer an amazing car-buying experience to all our customers, and meet their needs, we offer a wide selection of high quality used cars for sale, along with services such as insurance, finance and accessories, to ensure that all your car-buying needs are catered under one roof. Car-Showroom is your one address for quality pre-owned cars in India.
