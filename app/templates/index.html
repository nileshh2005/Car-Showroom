The famous sales guru, Zig Ziglar, always makes good points. The idea of “selling” is counter-intuitive to what it takes to gain trust with a client. Gaining trust is the only way you’re going to make the sale. The more you help without trying to sell a product, the more your customer will want to give you their money.
~ Zig Ziglar

If there is one thing an excellent salesperson hears all the time, it’s “no, thanks.” That sounds backwards, doesn’t it? Here’s the reason: making sales is not so much about closing every deal, as it is about getting front of the most people. Sales is a numbers game.
~ Brian Tracy

These car sales motivational quotes are meant to show the importance of understanding your customer’s pain points and developing trust. Those are two important steps in every sales process. The more you listen, the better you will understand how the customer wants to be sold. Do less talking and more paying attention, and your sales numbers will increase.
~ Roy Bartel
